Here Is The Ingredient List!

  1. Milk.
  2. Yeast. You can also use active dry yeast.
  3. Eggs. Large eggs are standard.
  4. Butter.
  5. Sugars. Granulated sugar goes in the dough. Brown sugar is in the filling.
  6. Salt.
  7. Flour. Bread flour will help the rolls have a fluffier, chewy but sturdier texture. If you don’t have bread flour, use all-purpose flour.
  8. Cinnamon. Use Saigon cinnamon in this filling.
  9. Heavy cream.
  10. Cream cheese. Full fat cream cheese is for the frosting.
  11. Powdered sugar.
  12. Maple or vanilla extract.
